James Brown

Born in Chesapeake, VA. He moved to Kodiak, AK at the age of 3, where he resided until 2002. He then moved to Norther Virginia for a year, before settling in Raleigh, NC in 2004. He has attended North Carolina A&T State University (Aggie Pride) since 2011. He has received is B.S. and M.S. in Computer Science and is currently enrolled in the PhD program. At the conclusion of his studies, he hopes to begin his career in academia as an Associate Professor.
